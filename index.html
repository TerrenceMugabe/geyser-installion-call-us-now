Battery-Operated Gas Geyser

Battery-operated gas geysers provide uninterrupted hot water for small to medium households, even during power outages, using battery-powered ignition. Ideal for showers, basins, kitchens, and baths, these units are compact and efficient.

  • Flow Rate: 9–20 L/min
  • Ignition: Battery-powered (2 x Type D Batteries, 3V)
  • Gas Type: LPG
  • Rated Gas Pressure: 2.8 kPa
  • Gas Consumption: 3.00 kg/hr
  • Water Pressure: 0.06–8.0 bar
  • Best for: 1–5 person households
  • Installation: Outdoor only, no flue pipe required
  • Note: Must be installed by a qualified gas installer for warranty validity

Included in Installed Price: Complete gas line installation, plumbing connection, Certificate of Compliance.

Not Included: Cage, cylinders, or gas.
